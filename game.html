Block Shooter

A retro block-form space shooter. Move your ship with the mouse or touch. Add enemy blocks to your ship by collecting their ghost forms. Every block color has a consistent ability.

How to play

  • Your ship follows your cursor or finger with no smoothing lag.
  • Black blocks fire simple pulses. Yellow is rapid fire. Orange is spread. Red is laser. Cyan is random. Purple is spiral. Gray is shield.
  • Destroy special blocks to spawn ghost pickups. Touch the ghost to attach the block to your ship.
  • If a player block is shot, it fades away. Lose the last block and the run ends.

Tips

Enemy formations grow more complex each level, but only a quarter or less of their blocks have special abilities. Music and sound are procedural, so the game works as a single HTML file.

Game Over

Your ship has been reduced to nothing. Run it back and build a stronger block formation.
